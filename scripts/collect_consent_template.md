# Consent Template for Audio/Video Data Collection

Use this template to collect informed consent for audio/video recording. Adapt to your institutional requirements.

Consent form checklist
- Purpose of recording: Explain clearly why you collect audio/video and how it will be used.
- Data types: audio, video, text transcription, derived features (e.g., embeddings).
- Storage: where data will be stored, encryption, retention period.
- Sharing: whether data or models will be shared, and with whom.
- Rights: ability to withdraw consent, contact details.

Metadata to collect (recommended)
- Participant ID (pseudonymized)
- Age range
- Gender (optional)
- Primary language
- Recording device type

Example short consent text
"By participating you consent to the recording of audio and video for research on multimodal language and emotion. Your data will be stored securely, used only for research, and may be shared in anonymized form. You may withdraw at any time by contacting [email]."
